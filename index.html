The White Stripes

together + touring 1997-2007

About

John Anthony 'Jack' White (born July 9, 1975) grew up in Detroit, Michigan. He was a fan of classical music as a child but in elementary school he started to listen to 60s rock and blues which would later influence him in the White Stripes. In 2005 Jack reported that prior to attending high school he was accepted into a Catholic seminary and had planned to become a priest, but abandoned this plan at the last minute because he had just gotten a new amplifier and didn’t think we was allowed to take it with him. Instead he attended a public high school where he played drums and trombone in the school band. There he also formed his first band and experimented with analog equipment and the associated working methods. Jack is known to be an eccentric type and has a history of creating mythology around his life events and circumstances. He lives in Nashville, Tennessee. Read more about Jack's lifehere.

Megan Martha 'Meg' White (born December 10, 1974) grew up in a Detroit suburb. In high school she was viewed as a shy, artistic type who decided to pursue a career as a chef rather than attending college. While working at a restaurant in Royal Oak, Michigan she met Jack with whom she dated and eventually married on September 21, 1996. One day on a whim Meg started playing Jack’s drumkit and so began the journey of embodying an integral part of what became the iconic sound of the White Stripes. To this day she is known for her minimalistic, almost primal yet childlike approach to playing the instrument. She is highly underrated as a drummer, as she both distinctive and innovative in her approach. Although Meg is incredibly private and very little is known about her whereabouts now, as of 2014 she still lived in Detroit. Read more about Meg's life here.
